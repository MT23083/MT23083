# SAVYA Privacy Policy

**App:** SAVYA (Save Your Assets)  
**Package:** `in.savya.wealth`  
**Effective date:** 22 July 2026  
**Contact:** [sarthaksharma618@gmail.com](mailto:sarthaksharma618@gmail.com)

This Privacy Policy explains how SAVYA (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use the SAVYA mobile app.

## 1. Who we are

SAVYA is a personal wealth tracking app. In Phase 1 it is **non-custodial**: we do not move money, hold funds, provide banking, or give regulated investment advice.

## 2. Information we collect

Depending on how you sign in and what you enter, we may process:

### Account / personal info
- Email address (email OTP or Google sign-in)
- Phone number (phone OTP)
- User IDs created by our auth provider

### Financial info you enter
- Assets, liabilities, transactions, goals, reminders, and related notes you choose to save (“other financial info”)

### Files and documents
- Document metadata and files you attach in the app (including on-device encrypted vault content where applicable)

### Local-only mode
You may use SAVYA offline/local without creating a cloud account. In that case, data stays on your device unless you later sign in and sync.

We do **not** currently collect precise/approximate location, contacts, SMS content, advertising IDs for ads, or sell personal data.

## 3. How we use information

We use the information above to:
- Create and secure your account
- Provide core app features (wealth tracking, sync, settings, account deletion)
- Maintain security and prevent abuse

We do **not** use this data for advertising or marketing in Phase 1.

## 4. Where data is stored / processors

Cloud accounts and synced data are hosted using **Supabase** (database and authentication) in the region configured for the project (India / `ap-south-1` when enabled).

Service providers process data **on our behalf** to run the app. They are not used as advertising partners.

## 5. Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal information.  
We do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.  
We may disclose information if required by law or to protect users/security.

## 6. Security

- Cloud traffic uses encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Sensitive on-device vault content is designed to be encrypted on device
- Access to cloud data is protected by authentication

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 7. Retention

We keep account and synced data while your account is active.  
When you delete your account (in-app or by email request), we delete associated cloud data as described in our [Account & data deletion](https://github.com/MT23083/MT23083/blob/main/ACCOUNT-DELETION.md) page, subject to short backup/legal retention where required.

## 8. Your choices

- Use local/offline mode without a cloud account
- Choose Google, email OTP, or phone OTP for cloud sign-in
- Delete account & cloud data in **Settings → Delete account & cloud data**
- Request deletion by email: **sarthaksharma618@gmail.com** (subject: `SAVYA account deletion request`)

## 9. Children’s privacy

SAVYA is intended for users **18+**. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 10. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Effective date” above will change when we do. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
