# SAVYA — Account & data deletion

**App:** SAVYA (Save Your Assets)  
**Package:** `in.savya.wealth`  
**Developer contact:** sarthaksharma618@gmail.com

## How to request deletion

### Option A — In the app
1. Open **SAVYA**
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Use **Delete account / data** (or equivalent delete control)
4. Confirm the request

### Option B — By email
Email **sarthaksharma618@gmail.com** with subject:

`SAVYA account deletion request`

Include:
- The email / phone / Google account used to sign in
- A short confirmation that you want the account deleted

We will process verified requests within **30 days**.

## What we delete
When your account is deleted, we remove or irreversibly anonymize associated personal data, including:

- Account identifiers (email / auth IDs)
- Profile and household membership records we control
- Synced assets, liabilities, transactions, reminders, goals, and document metadata stored on our servers

## What may be retained temporarily
- Security / fraud / audit logs needed for legal or safety reasons (retained only as long as required, then deleted or anonymized)
- Backups, which are purged on a rolling schedule after deletion

## Local device data
Data stored only on your device (including offline / encrypted vault files) can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling SAVYA.
