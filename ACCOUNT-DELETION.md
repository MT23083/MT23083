# SAVYA — Account & data deletion

**App:** SAVYA (Save Your Assets)  
**Package:** `in.savya.wealth`  
**Developer contact:** sarthaksharma618@gmail.com

## How to delete your account and data

### In the app (recommended)
1. Open **SAVYA**
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Delete account & cloud data**
4. Confirm **Delete forever**

This removes your SAVYA account from our servers (including synced wealth data and consents) and clears local data on that device.

### By email
If you cannot access the app, email **sarthaksharma618@gmail.com** with subject:

`SAVYA account deletion request`

Include the email / Google account / phone used to sign in. Verified requests are processed within **30 days**.

## What we delete
- Account identifiers (email / auth IDs)
- Profile and household records we control
- Synced assets, liabilities, transactions, reminders, goals, consents, and document metadata on our servers

## What may be retained briefly
- Security / audit logs required for legal or safety reasons, then deleted or anonymized
- Backups purged on a rolling schedule after deletion

## Local-only data
Encrypted vault files stored only on your device are removed when you delete in-app or uninstall SAVYA / clear app data.
